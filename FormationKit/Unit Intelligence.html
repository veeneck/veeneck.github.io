Unit Intelligence

  • FKIdleState

    When a unit is idle, its only job is to stay near the formation. So, it has to check if the leader is moving, and every so often it has to switch back to following to make sure it’s in the right place. For example, a physics object could bump it, and it needs to move back to formation.

  • FKFollowingState

    A unit will go into follow state on its own if out of position, or might be ordered by a squad. Following will just attempt to keep up with the desired standing position.
